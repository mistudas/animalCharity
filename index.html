About Us

Challenges Facing Our Animals :

In the wild, our majestic lions and tigers face unprecedented challenges. Habitat loss, poaching, conflict, and climate change threaten lions and tigers, causing population declines and an uncertain future.


How Your Donation Can Make a Difference:

Your donations help protect lions and tigers through habitat restoration, anti-poaching efforts, and community education. Together, we can ensure secure habitats and thriving populations for future generations.

Challenges Facing Our Animals :

Water bears face threats from habitat destruction, climate change, pollution, and the loss of their microhabitats due to environmental degradation.


How Your Donation Can Make a Difference:

Your donation helps conserve water bears' habitats, supports research, raises awareness, and ensures these resilient creatures thrive in their environments.

Challenges Facing Our Rhinos :

Rhinos face extinction from poaching, habitat loss, human conflicts, and climate change.


How Your Donation Can Make a Difference:

Your donation protects rhinos through anti-poaching, habitat conservation, and research, directly ensuring rhinos' survival.

Challenges Facing Our Animals :

Lions face habitat loss, human-wildlife conflict, poaching, prey depletion, and climate change, leading to declining populations across Africa and Asia.


How Your Donation Can Make a Difference:

Your donation supports anti-poaching efforts, habitat preservation, conflict mitigation, prey restoration, and research, ensuring the survival of lion populations.
